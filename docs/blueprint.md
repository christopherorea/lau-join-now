# **App Name**: ClarityVest AI

## Core Features:

- Informational Landing Page: Dynamically display all essential static content sections, including the hero area, 'what you get' benefits, and flexible pricing plans, leveraging Next.js for a performant UI.
- AI Financial Query Tool: Enable users to input their financial questions and receive AI-generated market analysis and mathematically-backed responses, providing clear, data-driven insights.
- Personalized Investment Insights Tool: Offer high-level, diversified asset allocation suggestions generated by an AI tool, guiding users with initial investment strategies tailored to general financial goals.
- Founders Program Waitlist: Integrate an external Google Forms iframe to facilitate user sign-ups for the exclusive '10 Fundadores' program, collecting interest with a special benefit offer.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A deep, professional blue (#173880), signifying confidence, stability, and trust in financial management. (HSL: 220, 70%, 30%)
- Background Color: A nearly pure white with a subtle cool undertone (#F6F8FA), maintaining a sense of pristine clarity and a clean, spacious feel. (HSL: 220, 15%, 98%)
- Accent Color (Clarity): A bright, clear sky blue (#60BBE3), reflecting the 'azul cielo' mentioned by the user to denote transparency and analytical insight. (HSL: 200, 70%, 65%)
- Accent Color (Growth): A soft, natural green (#3CA94D), drawing from the 'verde suave' accent requested by the user to symbolize growth and positive returns. (HSL: 140, 50%, 45%)
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, ensuring clear communication of complex financial information.
- Use minimalist line-art or geometric icons that convey themes of finance, growth, privacy, and digital expertise, consistent with a clean and uncomplicated aesthetic.
- Employ a 'Clean' and 'Minimal' layout with a strong grid structure, generous whitespace, and clear sectional separation to enhance readability and user focus on key information.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations, such as smooth scrolling transitions and discreet hover effects on interactive elements, to provide a polished user experience without distractions.
